Environmental health across Arizona

Arizona's rapid growth intersects with extreme heat, drought, wildfire smoke, and uneven access to clean air. This atlas maps publicly available indicators at the census-tract and county level so researchers and communities can see where environmental burdens concentrate.

Pipeline

R (sf, tidycensus, tigris, terra) → GeoJSON → Leaflet.js. Source scripts and data dictionary documented in the project repository.

Limitations

Tract-level data is modeled (EJScreen) or survey-based (ACS), not direct measurement. County climate values are area-weighted means. Fire perimeters reflect final mapped extent, not progression. This atlas is an exploratory tool, not a risk assessment.
